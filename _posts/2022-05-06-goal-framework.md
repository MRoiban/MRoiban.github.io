# Why habits are the key?

---

Our lives are defined by habits, going to sleep, showering, working out, all of these are habits that we do on a day by day basis.



When we plan on making a change in our lives, it is usually hard to do so and it the change doesn't last long. This can be because the change was to drastic, too big or simply that you didn't have the discipline and motivation to maintain the change.



To make a change, we must actively think about it and apply it, habits on the contrary are easily done and don't require much mental effort to start doing them. That is because our body got so used into doing that habit that it got assimilated by the body. Changes go against what our body it is used to, thus it is hard to maintain a change. 



Changes if maintained for a certain period of time can also become a habit, thus our body will assimilate the change and 'turn' it into a habit making it easier to do it again and again...



# How to build habits?

---

1. Have a clear and detailed goal in mind for what you want to achieve, this will help you plan on how to build the habit and also keep you on track.

2. After you decided on your goal, try to make a watered down version of it! So watered down that it becomes extremely easy to do, remember, our bodies are machines used to do the same thing everyday and don't like changes, thus by making the change really easy, the less friction we have.
   
   - By having a watered down version, we build a foundation for the original goal, making it easier for our body to get used to the bigger goal. Because it is already used to an easier version!
   
   - Example:
     
     - The goal: I want to be able to workout 3 times per week for a total of 3 hours.
     
     - Watered down version: Everyday do at least 2 minutes of exercise, just get your body moving and used to planned workouts!

3. Do the watered down version daily for at least 30 days so your body starts to get used to it and makes it a habit!

4. After that period start building up the watered down version towards your original goal by modifying it little by little, don't forget we want to add as little friction as possible to allow our bodies to adapt to the change!



Doing changes in our lives by using habits is extremely useful because it allows us to stick to the changes we want to do really easily.
