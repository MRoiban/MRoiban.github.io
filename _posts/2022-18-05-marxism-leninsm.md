Marxism-Leninism is a communist ideology that was the main communist movement of the 20th century. It was developed by Joseph Stalin in the 1920s based on the Marxism and Leninism. In today's age, Marxism-Leninism remains the official ideology of the ruling parties of China, Cuba, Laos and Vietnam.



# What is Marxism-Leninism?

---

Marxism-Leninism is a communist ideology which says that a two-stage communist revolution is needed in order to replace capitalism.

A vanguard party would seize the power in behalf of the proletariat and it would instore what is called a dictatorship of the proletariat where the state controls the means of production, counters revolutions and the bourgeoisie and promotes collectivism. All of which in order to build the foundations of the eventual communist society.
